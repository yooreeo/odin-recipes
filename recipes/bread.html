Sourdough Bread

Description

A hearty and tangy bread made with a sourdough starter.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sourdough starter
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sourdough starter, water, and salt to form a dough.
  2. Knead the dough until it is smooth and elastic.
  3. Let the dough rise in a warm place for several hours.
  4. Punch down the dough and shape it into a loaf.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the bread is golden.

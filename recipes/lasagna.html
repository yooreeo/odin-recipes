Lasagna

Description

A delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  3. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
